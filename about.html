Our Story

A Table Set with Purpose

Founded in 2018 by Chef Arjun Mehta and restaurateur Priya Kapoor, Aurum began as a quiet 20-cover space in South Delhi with one clear ambition: to create India's most refined dining experience.

Every decision — from the handmade ceramics to the 14-hour lamb jus — reflects an obsessive commitment to quality. We source directly from farms in Himachal, fishermen on the Malabar Coast, and cheese artisans in Auroville.

Today, Aurum is recognised among Asia's 50 Best Restaurants and has been awarded two stars by the Michelin Guide India since its inaugural edition.
