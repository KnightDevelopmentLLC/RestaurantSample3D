Our Philosophy

Born from a Love of Perfection

AURUM — Latin for gold — was founded in 2018 by Chef Aryan Mehra and restaurateur Kavya Nair with one guiding principle: food is the highest form of art.

Nestled on Marine Drive with sweeping views of the Arabian Sea, our 58-seat dining room is an intimate theatre where classical French technique collides beautifully with India's vibrant pantry. Each menu evolves with the seasons, each dish carries the fingerprint of its creator.

We believe in slowness — slow cooking, slow service, slow conversations. In a world addicted to speed, AURUM is your permission to linger.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

01
Obsessive Quality
Every ingredient passes through 7 quality checkpoints before it reaches our kitchen. We source directly from farms, fisheries, and vineyards we personally visit.
02
Artisanal Craft
Our bread is baked fresh each morning. Our butter is churned in-house. Our stocks simmer for 18 hours. There are no shortcuts in a kitchen dedicated to excellence.
03
Warm Hospitality
We train our team to anticipate needs before they are spoken. Service at AURUM feels like being cared for by an old friend who happens to know every great wine.
